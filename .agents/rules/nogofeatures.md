# No-Go Features Registry

This file tracks features that are proposed but rejected or marked as "no-go". These should not be proposed again in the future.

## 1. Feature 1: Dynamic Doppler Effect for High-Speed Flybys
* **Status:** No-Go
* **Description:** Shift the pitch of remote voices dynamically based on relative velocity vectors during high-speed ship flybys.

## 2. Feature 2: Helmet Damage & Life Support Failure Audio Effects
* **Status:** No-Go
* **Description:** Modulate respirator breathing speed and add static leaks/sparks to visor overlays when suit/helmet health is critical.

## 3. Feature 3: Planetary Weather Audio Modulation (Wind & Storms)
* **Status:** No-Go
* **Description:** Inject storm/wind howling noise and low-frequency gain tremors when characters are outside during active tempest weather.

## 4. Feature 4: AI Crew Voice Synthesis & Cockpit Readouts
* **Status:** No-Go
* **Description:** Integrate offline Text-to-Speech (TTS) to play spatialized AI warnings (shields, quantum status) from ship consoles.

## 5. Feature 5: Interactive Visor 3D Audio Visualizer (Waveform/Spectrogram)
* **Status:** No-Go
* **Description:** (Replaced by Visor Spectrogram Feature 6, but generic audio visualization of arbitrary environment sounds is rejected).

## 6. Feature 6: Custom Soundboards & Soundboard Degradation
* **Status:** No-Go
* **Description:** Let players trigger sound effects (alarms, warning horns) that undergo spatial and radio degradation.

## 7. Feature 7: Voice-Keyed Stress/Bio-Telemetry Scanner
* **Status:** No-Go
* **Description:** Perform voice stress analysis on remote player audio and post biometric alerts to HUD/Companion App.

## 8. Feature 8: Tactical Radio Direction Finder (RDF) Scanner
* **Status:** No-Go
* **Description:** Add distress beacons and RDF scanner to HUD to track approximate bearing of radio transmissions.

## 9. Feature 9: Dynamic Cockpit Reverb
* **Status:** No-Go
* **Description:** Applying room impulse responses and acoustic models dynamically to cockpit communications.

## 10. Feature 10: Ambient Cockpit Alarm Injection
* **Status:** No-Go
* **Description:** Mixing mathematical emergency sirens and rapid double-beep warning alarms dynamically into the outgoing microphone stream during ship warning states.

## 11. Feature 11: Radio Jamming / ECM
* **Status:** No-Go
* **Description:** Injecting server-side static noise or complete jamming of radio channels based on nearby hostile ships.

## 12. Feature 12: Proximity Occlusion by Vehicle Hatch
* **Status:** No-Go
* **Description:** Low-pass filter and attenuate proximity voice audio when the physical boundary/hatch of a ship is closed.

## 13. Feature 13: Speech-to-Text Operational Log Exporter
* **Status:** No-Go
* **Description:** Write transcribed/translated radio message texts into local operational files.

## 14. Feature 14: Visor HUD Sync for Intercom Glitches
* **Status:** No-Go
* **Description:** Render visual static flickering on the HUD overlay synchronized with audio intercom degradation events.

## 15. Feature 15: Acoustic Absorption by Suit/Armor Type
* **Status:** No-Go
* **Description:** Modify proximity dampening levels depending on light/medium/heavy armor worn by speakers.

## 16. Feature 16: Voice-Command Confirmation Sound FX
* **Status:** No-Go
* **Description:** Play a mechanical confirmation chime when a voice command is successfully parsed.

## 17. Feature 17: Personalization of HRTF Head-geometry
* **Status:** No-Go
* **Description:** Configure binaural audio ITD parameters using head-size and ear-spacing sliders.
